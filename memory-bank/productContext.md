# Product Context
> Educational Background: Computer Engineering (Istanbul Health and Technology University)

## Purpose
This portfolio serves as the digital business card and proof-of-work for Baran Çakı. It aims to bridge the gap between technical complexity (AI, Automation) and business value (Leads, Efficiency).

## Problem Solved
Potential clients or employers need to see not just *that* you can code, but *what* you can build and *how* it solves their problems. A standard resume or plain list of GitHub links is insufficient for high-value AI/Automation work. This site visualizes the "Digital Workers" Baran builds.

## User Experience Goals
- **Wow Factor**: The site should feel "alive" with animations and hover effects.
- **Clarity**: Complex backend processes (like scraping and enrichment) should be explained simply via visuals.
- **Premium Feel**: The design language (Void Black, Neon Purple, Cyan) conveys deep tech expertise and modern design sensibilities.
- **Easy Navigation**: Simple path from "Who is this?" to "What can he do?" to "Contact him".

## Target Audience
- **Business Owners/Executives**: Looking for automation solutions.
- **Recruiters/Hiring Managers**: Looking for AI Engineers.
- **Collaborators**: Other developers or tech professionals.
